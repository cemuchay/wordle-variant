Data Deletion Instructions

Variant values your privacy. According to Facebook's Platform Rules, we provide this instructions page to explain how you can request the deletion of your account and associated data from our servers.

What Data is Deleted?

  • Your profile information (Username, Avatar URL, and associated Facebook/Google metadata).
  • Your daily play history, game statistics, current streaks, and scoring records.
  • Your multiplayer challenge participation records.
  • Any chat messages sent in the global chat rooms (anonymized or removed).

How to Delete Your Data

You can request data deletion using either of the following two options:

Option 1: Request Deletion via Email
  • Send an email to our support team at [EMAIL_ADDRESS].
  • Use the subject line: Variant Data Deletion Request.
  • Please send the email from the address associated with your Variant account (or your Facebook-registered email address).
  • Your request will be processed, and all associated personal data will be purged within 72 hours.
    • Option 2: Delete Directly In-App
  • Open the Variant application.
  • Sign in to your account.
  • Open your Profile Settings (by clicking on your avatar or username).
  • Select **Delete Account** to instantly and permanently remove all your game data.
